Understanding how much raw materials we consume each year is key

Each year, we consume over 1,216 kilo tonnes of raw and other materials.

This is comparable to the weight of 8.5 cruise ships like the MSC Splendida.

We are playing an important role in making construction waste more circular

We create 165kt of annual consumption of meat and dairy products

Food waste is estimated to be 30% worldwide, and 20% in the Netherlands. Specifically for meat and dairy, the waste percentages are 10% and 8%, respectively.

12.4kt of electronics are consumed every year, it's about 100 times as heavy as a Blue Whale

The environmental impact of electronics has several dimensions. The CO2 impact of one laptop if equivalent to that of seven washing machines.
