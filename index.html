So what is Amsterdam doing with all this construction waste?

Construction is by far the biggest producer of waste, with only 7% of companies accounting for 70%.
However, it is very important for the economy. It also accounts for 10% of the employment.

The MRA (metropolitan area of Amsterdam) imports more construction waste than it exports. This means that because we focus on breaking down the waste so that it can be used as secondary raw materials. This is instead of disposing of it we can play an important, leading role.

OK Evi, but let me tell you about what we’re doing for food and organic waste!

There are two key initatives, the first is to transition away from animal proteins to vegetable. The objective is to consume a maximum of 40% animal and 60% plant-based proteins by 2050.

We’re also working on food waste, with the target to half food waste by consumers and businesses by 2030.
